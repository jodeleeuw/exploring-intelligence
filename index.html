Vassar Pre-College · Summer 2026 · Prof. Josh de Leeuw
Overview

In this course, we will explore artificial intelligence by thinking broadly about the concept of intelligence. What makes something intelligent? Is there something fundamentally different about "artificial" intelligence and "real" intelligence?

Each day opens with a framing question that we'll tackle through lecture and discussion. In the afternoons, we'll be hands-on in our robotics lab to explore the foundations of intelligent behavior.
